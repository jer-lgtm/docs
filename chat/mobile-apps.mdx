## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="App Won't Start">
  **Possible solutions:**
  
  - Restart your device
  - Clear app cache (Android) or reinstall (iOS)
  - Check for app updates
  - Ensure sufficient storage space
  - Verify internet connection
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Sync Issues">
  **If conversations aren't syncing:**
  
  - Check internet connection
  - Force close and reopen app
  - Sign out and sign back in
  - Check account status on web
  - Contact support if issues persist
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Performance Issues">
  **If the app is running slowly:**
  
  - Close other apps to free memory
  - Clear conversation cache
  - Update to latest app version
  - Restart device
  - Check available storage space
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Notification Problems">
  **If notifications aren't working:**
  
  - Check notification permissions
  - Verify Do Not Disturb settings
  - Check battery optimization settings
  - Ensure background app refresh is enabled
  - Test with a simple message
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
